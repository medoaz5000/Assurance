About us

What place does Sanlam Assurance occupy in Africa?

Sanlam is a benchmark financial group which is listed on the JSE Limited (Johannesburg Stock Exchange) and the Namibian Stock Exchange. Founded in 1918 as a life insurance company, the South Africa-based Sanlam Group later evolved into a diversified financial services company. In Morocco, Sanlam is the leader in the field of Non-Life insurance, N°1 in automobile and health. With more than 481 general agents, Sanlam has the most extensive exclusive network in Morocco, allowing it to ensure a very strong regional presence and to develop a policy of proximity with all of its customers.

What are the means used by Sanlam to provide its services?

The group develops complete and personalized financial solutions for the benefit of individual and institutional clients in all market segments, through all of its business divisions: Sanlam Personal Finance, Sanlam Emerging Markets, Sanlam Investments, Santam and the newly created Sanlam Corporate. The group's areas of expertise include insurance (life and general). It operates in insurance, financial engineering, retirement, trusts, wills, damages, asset management, risk management, capital market activities and wealth management.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • When can you change your auto insurance?

    You can change car insurance on the anniversary date of your contract, respecting the notice period. To compare your old contract with the Assur'Auto contract, make a free quote online and in a few minutes, on our simulator.

  • What is the difference between the third-party and all-risk car insurance formula?

    The all-risk insurance formula provides full coverage, conversely the third-party formula only covers your civil liability. The all-risk formula includes all mandatory and optional guarantees: fire, theft, glass breakage, driver and passenger protection, all-risk damage, etc.

  • What are the mandatory car insurance guarantees?

    The Civil Liability guarantee is a compulsory guarantee to insure your vehicle, with a minimum cover. It protects you in the event of an accident with a third party for which you are responsible. It covers damage caused to others (material damage and bodily injury).

  • Why take out car insurance?

    Car insurance is compulsory in Morocco for any owner of a motorized vehicle. Driving without insurance is illegal and exposes you to penalties. In the event of an accident with a third party without insurance, you will have to reimburse the costs which are your responsibility.

  • How much does car insurance cost?

    The price of your car insurance depends on several criteria: the profile of the driver, the vehicle, the desired coverage. The seniority and background of the driver are taken into account, with the characteristics of the vehicle (brand, power, seniority value) and the coverage formula (third or minimum formula, intermediate formula, all-risk formula).

  • Can you cancel your car insurance?

    You can cancel your car insurance on the expiry date of your annual contract. You must inform your insurance company by respecting the notice mentioned in your contract.
